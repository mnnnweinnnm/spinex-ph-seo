Responsible Gambling

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Gambling should be entertainment, not a way to make money. If you feel you may have a problem, please seek help. In the Philippines, you can reach out to the organizations below for free, confidential support.

Signs You May Need a Break

  • Spending more money or time than you planned
  • Chasing losses — trying to win back what you lost
  • Neglecting work, family, or personal responsibilities
  • Borrowing money to gamble
  • Lying to friends or family about how much you gamble
  • Feeling anxious or depressed when you cant gamble

If one or more apply, consider taking a break or setting deposit limits.

Practical Tools

  • Set a deposit limit — most platforms, including SPINEX, allow daily, weekly, or monthly caps. Use them.
  • Set a time limit — use a phone alarm to track how long you have been playing.
  • Take regular breaks — step away every 30-60 minutes to reset your perspective.
  • Never gamble with money you need — rent, bills, or savings should never be used for gambling.
  • Keep a gambling budget — treat any winnings as a bonus, not income.

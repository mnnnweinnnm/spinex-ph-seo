Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

What We Collect

SPINEX News is a static informational site. We do not operate accounts, process transactions, or collect personal data. Our hosting provider may log standard server access data (IP address, pages visited, browser type) for site security and performance purposes.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or advertising trackers. The site may use a minimal session cookie for navigation purposes only. No personal information is stored.

Third-Party Services

Our site uses Google Fonts (hosted by Google) and may link to SPINEX and game provider sites. These third parties have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies when visiting external sites.

Cloudflare

This site is served through Cloudflare, which processes HTTP traffic according to its own privacy policy. See Cloudflare Privacy Policy.

Affiliate Links

Affiliate links on this site go directly to SPINEX. Any data collection or account creation on SPINEX is governed by SPINEX's own privacy policy.

Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data beyond standard server logs maintained by third-party providers, there is limited personal data held by this site. For Cloudflare-processed data, contact Cloudflare directly. For data related to your SPINEX account, contact SPINEX support.
